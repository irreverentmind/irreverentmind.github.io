About The Work of Byron Katie

The Work is a method of identifying and questioning the thoughts that cause stress in our lives. It's a way to end suffering. It's simple and powerful and it works for everyone whose mind is open to it.

Using 4 simple questions and Turn Arounds, The Work slices through confusion to help identify what's hurting you, what's underneath the depression, anger or addictions. It has a profound effect on your whole life, and even on family members and others around you.

As a fully trained facilitator of The Work of Byron Katie, Judy guides you through the process of self discovery in hour-long sessions, in person, by phone or Skype. Whether it's one-on-one, couples, family or corporate sessions, anywhere you are in the world, you and Judy can work together to help you discover how close, how possible your peace can be.
