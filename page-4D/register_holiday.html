Come Re-find your Ho Ho Ho!
Make the holidays more merry.

Join Judy Cohen

for a
Spur of the Moment Pop-Up Webinar

Does the holiday season make you jangle more than jingle?

If traffic, money worries, family triggers, company (or lack thereof), over-indulging, and lengthy to-do lists make it hard to find the comfort and joy…

If it might be nice to find a bit of mind quieting in the midst of the hubbub…

If your Ho Ho Ho has got up and gone…

Then join me for a special, last-minute pop-up webinar designed to help you re-find the holiday spirit.

Whether you're Scrooge, a Maccabee or Cindy Lou Who, bring your questions, trigger situations, and "Aaauugh!" feelings. Come remember what you really are before putting on a happy face with people who believe that you're something else entirely.

Give yourself the gift of a holiday touch-base and reality check, before spending what seems like real-time in the land of what's not really happening.

And of course, come have some fun for a change too.
