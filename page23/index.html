Small Groups with Judy

A quietly effective alternative for a lighter life


"Wow these groups are powerful." —Ireland
 
This is a place where heads come loose.*

If you're anything like most folks who contact Judy Cohen, you've tried various forms of inquiry, therapy, meditation and mindfulness and found that you're still not experiencing the freedom you've been hoping for.

Maybe that's because one can't use thought to solve situations which thought created in the first place. Despite what it says, mind is not interested in helping anyone
get free of itself.

Which is why what Judy Cohen does is so different.

Deliberately tricking the mind with its own game, Judy subtly and intentionally re-routes it into a quieter, less-stressed experience of life.

In her Small Groups, Judy provides a guided space for thought to detangle and relax its grip. Ego / self / mind is slyly kept preoccupied so that quickly, almost imperceptibly, from behind the story a whole new way of experiencing can begin to emerge.

Judy has a large bag of tools. Group participants often say, "Wait, what just happened?" as depression, anxiety, lack of motivation and sense of being broken drift away and go poof, of their own accord.

“I love this group because I don’t even have to do anything and
it infiltrates in a very subversive way.”

Life starts to feel better. More enjoyable. And for most, that is welcome relief and a very good thing.

In Small Groups with Judy you get ongoing clarity and support to transcend issues and situations of the dream. And as a result, Dream-you gets to feel better, think clearer, see truer.

"I'm feeling at home with myself in a way I have never felt, ever."

Requirements for Participation
Judy’s groups are designed to support you with repetition, reminders, consistency, so that the mind gremlins can eventually relax their grip more frequently. That’s why there are these Group requisites:

vvvvvv• Twice monthly meetings
vvvvvv• Three-month minimum commitment
vvvvvv• 30-day notice if leaving group after 3 months

This provides needed reminders and repetition for learning when mind oh-so-conveniently forgets, and enables consistency and security for you and all participants.

Small Groups with Judy are not for everyone. Judy has some requirements too, to make sure there’s a good fit for all. So contact her to discuss your eligibility, fees and availability.

Once you're accepted:

vvvvvv• Month-to-month membership after 3 months
vvvvvv• No increase in fee for entire duration of your membership
vvvvvv• Recording of meeting available if you cannot attend in person
vvvvvv• Possibility of attending different group for missed session
