Advanced Class:
UnDoing Procrastination

A continued deep-dive class for previous participants of UnDoing Procrastination

By now you’ve gotten a good sense of how to UnDo Procrastination.

You’re feeling like things are becoming unblocked but there’s still more you’d like to play with.

This class is for you.

Like the beginner class, this one will continue to UnDo obstacles- thoughts, fears, paralysis and blocks.

Bring your list of big and small projects. We will target your specific situations, work individually in class, support each other, and get it done.

You’ve probably noticed that in the process of focusing on procrastination, your whole world can crack open.

So it’s about a whole lot more than procrastination.

Like the first-level UnDoing Procrastination class, this is a no-pressure, no-blame, practical class.

All “I don’t want to”s are welcome.

Permission to not want to act.

And permission to act, also.

And as you’ve seen, you might find things start to get done anyway.



Advanced UnDoing Procrastination
a 4-week online class


We will meet 4 times. Exact dates and time will be determined by participants’ schedules.
