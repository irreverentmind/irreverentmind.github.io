depression, anxiety, panic attacks, fear, un-safety, doom, sadness, grief & despair

"Sad. Scared. So tired of fighting myself.
Help please."
 

Everyone knows people aren't supposed to be so sad. No one should have to live with anxiety either.

Even though some level of depression or anxiety is so common these days it's like an epidemic.

Still, it does feel like there's something not-right with you in particular.

You're blue. Afraid. Feeling alone. Life isn't much fun. It's hard to get out of bed in the mornings.
 
It could range from just a mild hum of unease and sadness to full-on inability to function, panic attacks, and thoughts of suicide.

Or you may have had a kind of awakening experience, and now somehow you can't deal with anything, because… well, really what's the point?

No matter how intense or mild your depression or anxiety is, one thing seems sure- with all the spiritual work and inquiry you've done, thought insists you should know better by now than to still be stuck like this.

Turns out all that self-help self-improvement actually… hasn't. Or at least it's not been integrated into life in a meaningful way.

Of course you want this to stop.

Who wouldn't? It's natural to want help and solutions.

Though since you've already tried lots of approaches and methods, maybe it makes sense to try something different.

Maybe there's a way to integrate all you've learned and experienced in the non-dual and inquiry and meditation worlds, and find a practical way that can help you just… feel better and be up for life. If so…

Welcome to Undoing Depression & Anxiety, another
blow-the-mind class in the Live What You Know series.

This one-of-a-kind class will help you learn new ways to undo depression, anxiety, and the stories about what it all means, about you.
 
Its non-dual approach hits personal suffering where it begins. You will undo faulty thinking and clear a path out.

You will learn ways to connect with emotional peace, regardless what is happening in any external situation.
 
And you just might find that peace comes to visit much more often.

“This class was mind-blowing.”  

This 2-hour online class covers:

  • Something is very wrong in the world and with you
  • What can you do? The search for solutions, control and hope
  • Blaming yourself. Hating yourself
  • Dealing with the ache of past and fear of future: You shouldn’t have done those things. Disaster is coming. There is no hope.
  • Permanence: This will never change
  • How to integrate on your own
