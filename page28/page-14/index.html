The Live What You Know Series

Integrate spiritual learning, inquiry & personal work
with everyday life

It's hard to remember what you know when you're busy living life. It's hard to care a whit about consciousness or awakening or whether the self does or does not exist, when something painful is going on.

Life continues to present what seems to be less-than-ideal situations. Whether it's family, job, money, love, mood, politics, natural disasters or even addiction to spiritual seeking…

We can often feel "caught up" in circumstances and forget, or not know how, to live what we've learned.

The Live What You Know classes help integrate all that hard-earned understanding with the day to day. Because thankfully it is possible to jive your spiritual understandings with daily life.

That integration enables the same circumstances to feel quite different, often even better. You may be able to enjoy a more satisfying life experience no matter what challenges come your way.
