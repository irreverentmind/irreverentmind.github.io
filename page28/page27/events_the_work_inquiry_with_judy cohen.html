Teleclass: When You Think You’re A Failure

"I am not good enough." "I have failed." "I will never succeed."

If thoughts like these are familiar companions in your life, join us for a 5-class Teleclass to untangle the painful stories about failure and success that hold you back.

When your thinking has you convinced of failure in areas such as:
• relationships or the lack of them
• as a parent
• work or money
• body & health
• spirituality & personal growth
• as a human being,
you have to live out those thoughts. The resulting pain, guilt and shame create further proof of failure and perpetuate the cycle.

Expose beliefs about failure and success that live under the surface of everyday life and affect your confidence, ability, identity and peace.

Discover who you would be, free of the chains of failure and success beliefs.

Class 1: "I'm a failure. Really."
Class 2: Who said so?
Class 3: Suffering by comparison
Class 4: If they knew the truth about me
Class 5: I want that life

Teleclass: Rebellion, Authority and Inquiry

"They have no right." "I won't stand for it." "Don't tell me what to do!"

Do buttons get pushed when people tell you what to do? Do you find yourself living out those pushed buttons, creating consequences in your jobs, in learning environments, in relationships? Or maybe you get small and afraid and child-like with people of authority?

What if there was another way? What if there was a way to maintain your integrity and independence without war or fear, even in a world where people do sometimes tell you what to do?

Come join us to inquire into rebellion against authorities in your life, and discover your true options, power and equilibrium, no matter what others may do.

Don't Tell Me What To Do! Rebellion, Authority and Inquiry
Class I: People in Charge: Who are they?
Class II: Who do they think they are?
Class III: Don't Tell me what to do.
Class IV: Obedient people.
Class V: You can't make me.
