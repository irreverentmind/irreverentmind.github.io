Online class:

The Hope In Hopelessness
Peace, hidden where we'd least expect.

Contact Judy for info on next class

Think about all the areas in your life that aren't what they should be.

Maybe you're lacking success or confidence, or feeling alone and unappreciated.

Perhaps you're depressed, anxious and unable to function, or you're easily angered in too many situations.

Maybe you drink too much.

Maybe you should be awakened by now and it just hasn't happened.

Maybe you're sick and tired of being sick and tired.

The list of improvements needed can go on and on, can't it?

So many behaviors and situations where what you or life does is unacceptable and needs fixing.

And then consider all the measures you've taken over the years to make things better or right- all that meditation, the "allowing life to be as it is", the therapy, workshops, satsangs and following various people who seem to have the answers. All those inquiries!

Look at the time, energy, money invested in attempting to fix. 

And here you are, still unfixed.

Yikes, huh?

So now what?

What if there is no answer to "now what?" What if... this is it?

What if you're never going to be more confidant, never going to feel less alone, never going to fix your feeling state or behaviors? 

What if this is it and there's nothing, truly nothing, that can be done?

Maybe your reaction to that question is sadness, anger or hopelessness. You may be thinking "This is terrible! I can't live with this!"  

Yes, if this is it, this is not how it should be either, right? 

But maybe it's time, instead of all that trying to fix and eliminate hopelessness and the various stories of our lives, that we actually tried something different and finally turned toward it all.

Because what if what you're seeking is actually right there IN the hopelessness?
